🏃 Running Warm-Up (8–10 min)

1. Brisk Walk (5 min)

  • Walk briskly before every run.
  • Allow your heart rate to gradually increase.

2. Ankle Mobility

  • Knee-Over-Toe Ankle Rocks: 10 each side
  • Ankle Circles: 10 clockwise & 10 counterclockwise each foot

3. Calf Activation

  • Bodyweight Calf Raises: 10 reps
  • Bent-Knee Calf Raises: 10 reps

4. Dynamic Leg Mobility

  • Leg Swings (Front/Back): 10 each leg
  • Leg Swings (Side-to-Side): 10 each leg

5. Lower Body Activation

  • Bodyweight Squats: 10 reps
  • Glute Bridges: 10 reps (2-second squeeze at the top)

6. Start Easy

  • Run the first 5–10 minutes at an easy conversational pace.
  • Ignore pace early and focus on relaxed breathing.
  • Gradually settle into your normal easy pace.
